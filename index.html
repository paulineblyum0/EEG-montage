The source recontruction shown below is the result of the two-tailed permutation test comparing male and female magnitudes of activation in the 1000ms post-stimulus recording period. Positive values show voxels with significantly greater activation in females and negative values show voxels with significantly greater activation in males (a=0.05). Invalid scale indicates there were no voxels that had significantly different values between males and females at that particular timepoint.
